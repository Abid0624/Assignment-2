What Happens in Class

01

Rowing

Rowing in training is a full-body workout that targets multiple muscle groups, including the legs, core, and arms. It's an excellent cardiovascular exercise that enhances endurance, burns calories, and improves overall strength. Rowing also promotes coordination and balance, making it an ideal choice for building fitness and achieving a lean, toned physique.

02

Cardio

Cardio training is essential for improving heart health, boosting endurance, and burning calories. It involves activities like running, cycling, or jumping rope, which elevate your heart rate and enhance lung capacity. Regular cardio workouts help reduce the risk of chronic diseases, increase energy levels, and support weight management, contributing to overall fitness and well-being.

03

Strength Training

Strength training is crucial for building muscle, increasing bone density, and enhancing metabolic rate. By using weights, resistance bands, or bodyweight exercises, it targets specific muscle groups to improve strength and endurance. Regular strength training supports joint health, improves posture, and helps in maintaining a healthy weight, leading to a stronger, more resilient body.
